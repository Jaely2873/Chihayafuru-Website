But first, Let's do a quick dive into what Chihayafuru is!

Chihayafuru is a Japanese manga series by Yuki Suegetsu. It follow high school student Chihaya Ayase and her journey to become the queen of Karuta. The series was adapted into an anime in 2011 and ran for three seasons. It received three live action films with the first releasing in 2016. The series greatly increased the popularity for karuta. By 2025, the series had 29 million copies in circulation
