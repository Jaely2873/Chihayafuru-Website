About

Chihayafuru is a Japanese manga series by Yuki Suetsugu. It follows a young girl named Chihaya and her goal of one day becoming the queen of karuta.

The series ran from December 28th 2007 to August first 2022. It has 247 chapters collected into 50 volumes. By February 2025 the series had 29 million copies in circulation
